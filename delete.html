Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Please read these terms carefully before proceeding with the deletion of your Google account.

1. Account Deletion Process

When you request account deletion, the following process will be initiated:

1
Immediate Effect
Account access suspended immediately
2
30-Day Grace Period
Account recoverable within 30 days
3
Permanent Deletion
Complete data removal after 30 days

2. Data That Will Be Deleted

The following data will be permanently removed from your account:

  • All emails, contacts, and calendar events in Gmail
  • Photos and videos stored in Google Photos
  • Files and documents in Google Drive
  • YouTube channel, videos, and comments
  • Google Play purchases and download history
  • Search history and location data
  • Chrome bookmarks and browsing data
  • All other Google services data

3. Services That Will Be Affected

Account deletion will result in loss of access to:

  • Gmail and Google Workspace services
  • Google Drive and Google Photos
  • YouTube (including subscriptions and playlists)
  • Google Play Store purchases
  • Google Pay and payment methods
  • Android device backup and sync
  • Google Assistant and Home devices
  • All third-party apps using Google Sign-In

4. Before You Delete

We strongly recommend the following actions before proceeding:

  • Download a copy of your data using Google Takeout
  • Update sign-in methods for other accounts and services
  • Cancel any active subscriptions or memberships
  • Inform contacts of your email address change
  • Transfer ownership of shared files and documents
  • Save important emails and documents locally
  • Update your primary email on financial and important accounts

5. Recovery Period

You have a 30-day grace period to recover your account:

  • Account can be restored within 30 days of deletion request
  • After 30 days, recovery is not possible
  • Some data may be permanently deleted immediately for security reasons
  • Recovery requires identity verification

6. Legal and Compliance

Please note the following legal considerations:

  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes
  • Deletion may be delayed due to ongoing legal processes
  • Certain business or institutional accounts may have different policies
  • Data shared with others may remain accessible to those users

7. Alternative Options

Consider these alternatives before permanent deletion:

  • Temporary account deactivation
  • Deleting specific services instead of entire account
  • Changing privacy settings and data collection preferences
  • Using account activity controls to limit data collection

8. Terms and Conditions

By proceeding with account deletion, you acknowledge and agree that:

  • You understand the permanent nature of this action
  • You have backed up any important data
  • You will not hold Google responsible for data loss
  • You understand the impact on connected services and devices
  • You have read and understood all terms outlined above
